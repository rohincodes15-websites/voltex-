Who We Are

FORGED IN STEEL & LIGHTNING

VOLTEX is a FIRST Tech Challenge team built on engineering passion, precision, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. Like our logo — a hammer crackling with electricity — we combine timeless fundamentals with cutting-edge innovation.

We operate across three core disciplines: Hardware Engineering, Software Development, and Community Outreach. Every subsystem we build, every line of code we write, and every student we inspire is a step toward the World Championship.

What Drives Us

OUR CORE PILLARS

Six disciplines that define how VOLTEX builds, competes, and grows.

01
💻

Software

Our code is the intelligence behind VOLTEX. From autonomous routines in C++ to precise PID control and sensor fusion — we engineer software that makes good robots great.

02
⚙️

Hardware

Every mechanism has a purpose. Our drivetrains, intake systems, and lift assemblies are engineered for speed, reliability, and competition-level performance.

03
🌍

Outreach

We build more than robots — we build community. VOLTEX spreads STEM awareness, mentors the next generation, and represents our school with pride.

04
📖

Notebook

Our engineering notebook documents every decision, test, and iteration. We treat our process documentation with the same rigor we apply to our robot design.

05
🤝

Teamwork

VOLTEX is just getting started, but our culture is set: every member has a voice, every role matters. As we grow our roster, that foundation will drive us forward.

06
🏆

Competition

We're building VOLTEX with one ambition: to compete and win. Every practice run, every match, every tournament is a step toward proving what this team is capable of.
